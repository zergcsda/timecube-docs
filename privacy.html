Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 27, 2025

TimeCube ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the TimeCube mobile application.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal data on our own servers
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not track your location, browsing history, or usage patterns

1.2 Data Stored on Your Device

All your app data is stored locally on your device and, if enabled, synced via Apple iCloud:

  • Task lists and schedules
  • Habit records and statistics
  • Points and card collections
  • Bank balance and transaction history
  • App preferences and settings

2. Data Usage

The data generated within the app is used solely for providing the core features:

  • Managing your daily tasks and routines
  • Tracking your habit progress and streaks
  • Operating the gamified reward system
  • Synchronizing data across your Apple devices via iCloud

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Data stored on your device is protected by iOS's built-in security features, including device encryption.

3.2 iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync:

  • Your data is stored securely in Apple's cloud infrastructure
  • Data is protected by Apple's security protocols and your Apple ID credentials
  • Data may be stored in Apple's data centers worldwide

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

TimeCube is designed to be suitable for children. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from children
  • We do not display targeted advertising
  • All data remains within the user's private iCloud environment or on their device
  • The app includes parental controls (PIN protection) for sensitive settings

5. European Union Users (GDPR)

5.1 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Performance of contract: To provide the app's core functionality
  • Legitimate interests: To maintain and improve the app

5.2 Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data through the app or iCloud
  • Rectification: Edit your data directly within the app
  • Erasure: Delete your data by deleting the app or clearing iCloud data
  • Data portability: Export your data via iCloud
  • Object: You may stop using the app at any time
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

5.3 Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you use the app. When you delete the app, local data is removed. iCloud data can be managed through your Apple ID settings.

5.4 International Data Transfers

When using iCloud sync, your data may be transferred to and stored in Apple's data centers located outside your country of residence. Apple provides appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

6. California Users (CCPA)

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
  • You have the right to know what data we collect (none on our servers)
  • You have the right to delete your data (through the app or iCloud)

7. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

TimeCube offers optional subscription services (TimeCube Pro):

  • Payment is processed by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not receive or store your payment information
  • Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID settings

8. Third-Party Services

TimeCube uses the following Apple services:

  • CloudKit: For data synchronization (Apple's privacy policy applies)
  • StoreKit: For in-app purchases (Apple's privacy policy applies)
  • WidgetKit: For home screen widgets (no external data transfer)

We do not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or social media SDKs.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Effective Date" above.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
